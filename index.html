Creating opportunities for students and connecting them with their dream jobs

Empowering students and simplifying process with a centralized, web-based portal.

We help to streamline the placement process, provide a platform for students and employers, and facilitate the university's goal of ensuring successful career opportunities for its students.

We provide a platform for the university students to find internships, job opportunities, and career guidance.

We aim to provide career guidance and counselling to the students by interacting with alumni.

We allow employers to post job vacancies, view student profiles, shortlist and contact the students.

We also help the university to maintain a database of the students and their placement status.
